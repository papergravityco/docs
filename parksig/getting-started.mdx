## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A ParkSig account
- API credentials from the dashboard

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title='Create your account'>
    Sign up at [papergravity.com](https://papergravity.com) and navigate to the
    ParkSig app.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Configure your first sign'>
    Follow the setup wizard to register your parking location and sign details.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Go live'>
    Publish your sign configuration and start managing permits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
