The **Parking Locations** page lists every location in your account. From here you can review availability, check rates and features, and navigate to a location's details or settings.

## Viewing locations

Each location appears as a card showing:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | The label you gave the location. |
| **Address** | The full street address. |
| **Availability** | A color-coded bar and space count (available / total). |
| **Starting at** | The location's hourly rate. |
| **Features** | Up to three feature badges, with a count shown if there are more. |

### Availability colors

The availability bar changes color based on how full the location is.

| Color | Availability |
|---|---|
| Green | More than 60% of spaces available |
| Amber | 31–60% of spaces available |
| Red | 30% or fewer spaces available |

## Opening a location

From any location card, click **View Details** to open the full location page. This shows permits, activity, and additional configuration.

## Editing a location

If you have permission to manage locations, an **Edit** button appears on each card. Click it to update the location's name, address, rates, features, or flyer text.

## Adding a location

Users with location management permissions see an **Add Location** button in the top-right corner. Click it to create a new parking location.

If no locations exist yet, the page shows a prompt to add your first one.
