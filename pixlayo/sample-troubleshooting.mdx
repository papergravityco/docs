## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='Images are not syncing to my integration'>
    1. Verify the integration is still connected under **Settings > Integrations**.
    2. Check that the folder permissions allow Pixlayo to write to the destination.
    3. Review the sync log for error messages.

    If the issue persists, disconnect and reconnect the integration.

  </Accordion>

<Accordion title='Uploads are failing'>
  - Confirm the file does not exceed the 500 MB size limit. - Supported formats:
  JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, SVG, AVIF. - Check your network connection and try
  again.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Team members cannot access a workspace'>
    Ensure the user has been invited and has accepted the invitation email. Check their assigned role under **Settings > Team**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Processed images look incorrect'>
    Review the transformation rules applied to the asset. Go to **Workflows** and inspect the rule configuration. You can preview the output before saving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Contact support

If you cannot resolve your issue, reach out to the PaperGravity support team at [support@papergravity.com](mailto:support@papergravity.com).
