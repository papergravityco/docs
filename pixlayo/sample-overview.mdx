## What is Pixlayo?

Pixlayo is a visual content platform that allows teams to create, manage, and distribute image-based content at scale.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title='Asset management' icon='images'>
    Organize and tag all your visual assets in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Automated workflows' icon='rotate'>
    Define rules to process, resize, and publish images automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Team collaboration' icon='users'>
    Invite teammates and assign role-based permissions.
  </Card>
  <Card title='Analytics' icon='chart-bar'>
    Track asset usage and performance across channels.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

1. Upload assets to your Pixlayo workspace.
2. Apply tags, metadata, and processing rules.
3. Distribute assets to connected channels through integrations.
