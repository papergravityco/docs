## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='What is ParkSig?'>
    ParkSig is a parking sign permit management platform that lets you digitally manage permits for your parking locations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='How do I reset my password?'>
    Go to the login page and click **Forgot password**. You'll receive a reset link at your registered email address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='How am I billed?'>
    ParkSig uses a subscription model billed monthly or annually. See your plan details under **Settings > Billing**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Can I cancel at any time?'>
    Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the dashboard. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
